Money or Love
===============
2D puzzle game made by using Crafty. 
The man needs to collect the gold and 
the woman needs to collect the heart.
  
HOW TO PLAY:
	
	W-A-S-D or arrow keys: Move
	R: Restart the level
	
	Collect the gold with the boy; and 
	collect the heart with the girl, 
	then putting them in any box. 
	
	In some levels, avoiding from lava 
	is needed.
